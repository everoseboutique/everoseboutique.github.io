Meet the Founder

Hi, my name is Cruz Keller, and I am the founder of Everose.

I was born in New Jersey and New York, and I’ve always been inspired by creativity, beauty, and self-expression. I love to dance, spend time with my family, and dream up new ideas.

Everose was created from my love for elegance, softness, and confidence. I wanted to build a fragrance brand that feels emotional, beautiful, and meaningful — something that people can connect memories and feelings to.

Every Everose scent is inspired by a season, a mood, and a moment. This brand is not just about perfume, but about becoming your most confident and graceful self.
